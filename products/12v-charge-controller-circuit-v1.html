12V Charge Controller Circuit V1

  • Battery Charge Controller Circuit Suitable For 12V Battery
  • Up to 2000 watt 220v load Capable
  • Up to 10 Amp 220v load Capable
  • Up to 200-400 AH Battery Charging Capable
  • Very Low Current Consumption
  • In Built Power Saving Method
  • Auto Cut off main charging transformer on full charge
  • Efficient charging system
  • Overcharge & Discharge protection
  • Easy installation with compact design
  • Accurate full charge signal with long durability
BDT 220 BDT 320

Quantity

The 12V Charge Controller Circuit V1 is a reliable and efficient solution for managing 12V lead-acid batteries. Designed for both home and industrial use, this smart charge controller protects your battery from overcharging, discharging, and overload damage, ensuring longer battery life and stable power supply.

Built with power-saving technology and an auto cut-off feature that disconnects the main charging transformer once the battery is full, this controller helps reduce energy consumption while maintaining accurate charging. Its compact design makes installation simple, while the durable PCB and high-quality components guarantee long-lasting performance.

Whether you’re powering solar panels, inverters, or 12V DC systems, this charge controller offers safe and efficient charging for batteries up to 200–400AH. It is suitable for 2000W 220V loads and supports up to 10A charging current, making it an excellent choice for both DIY enthusiasts and professionals looking for a dependable 12V battery charge controller.

  • Circuit Input Voltage: 18V DC (9v transformer)
  • Charger/Power Supply: Up to 10A 220V Charger AC Input
  • Max Current: 10A
  • Battery: Up to 400 AH
  • Battery Type: Lead Acid Battery
  • Material: PCB + High-grade components
  • Dimensions: 4.06inc x i.93inc
