Luffy's Coffee Shop - Digital VR Shop

Published: September 15, 2025 Md. Al-Amin

Project Description

Luffy's Coffee Shop is an immersive Digital VR simulation experience built using Unity, designed to showcase a fully interactive virtual coffee shop environment. Users can explore the shop, interact with staff characters, place orders, and experience the atmosphere of a cozy and lively café. Whether for promotional use, training, or virtual tourism, this project brings hospitality to life in a digital space—offering a unique blend of realism and engagement through virtual reality.
