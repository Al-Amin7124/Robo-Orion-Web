Shadow Ran 2D – Endless Ninja Runner Game

Published: March 5, 2026 Md. Al-Amin

Game Overview

Shadow Ran 2D is a fast-paced 2D endless runner game where a skilled ninja runs continuously through a minimalistic environment filled with dangerous obstacles. Inspired by the popular Chrome offline dinosaur game, this browser-based experience challenges players to react quickly and survive as long as possible.

Your mission is simple — jump at the right time, avoid crashing into obstacles, and achieve the highest score. The longer you survive, the faster the game becomes, increasing the difficulty and excitement.

Play Shadow Ran 2D Online

📱 On smaller screens, scroll right inside the game frame to find the fullscreen button.

Click the full-screen icon for the best gameplay experience. Challenge yourself and beat your highest score!

How to Play

  • PC: Press Spacebar or Up Arrow to jump.
  • Mobile: Tap anywhere on the screen to jump.
  • Avoid hitting obstacles — one crash means Game Over.
  • Press the Reset button to play again.
  • Pause the game anytime using the pause control.
